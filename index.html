Studio

My partner and I co-founded “orangutan matter” studio, our mission is driven by the conviction that a game is not just an entertainment medium but a profound human activity and a cultural product. Our mission is clear: to craft games that serve as expressive canvases of the human condition and emotions, blending art and intellectual appreciation seamlessly.

Our studio has earned the trust of major industry leaders like Microsoft and Sony, as well as collaborating with other studios. With cutting-edge technologies such as Unity, C languages, and JavaScript libraries, we are dedicated to creating visually captivating and emotionally resonant gaming and digital interactive experiences.

Each game & digital experince we create resonates deeply with users while pushing the boundaries of art and technology.

I'm thrilled to share with you Unleaving! Unleaving marks orangutan matter's debut game—a story-driven puzzle platformer where every frame is a literal hand painting.

It's now available on Steam, Epic Store and Xbox and soon coming to Playstation.

"Toronto-made Unleaving is one of the most stunning games I’ve ever played," says @MobileSyrup.
